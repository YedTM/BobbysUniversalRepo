Documentation for Phase Two

All images except for the top Bounty Hunter image come from Darkest Dungeon II directly or from the Darkest Dungeon Wiki.

The top Bounty Hunter image comes from here.

The information summarized in the Backstory, Skills, and Description sections came from Darkest Dungeon II and from the Darkest Dungeon Wiki.

For the responsive design of Phase 2, I used the Phone layout found in Phase 1's documentation. I decided to avoid using the Tablet format as I felt that it would take up too much time that could have been used for other things.

The same fonts and resources from Phase 1 were used in Phase 2.

The intended audience for this website is beginners to Darkest Dungeon II and normal Darkest Dungeon II players. The information present on the page allows beginners to get a feel for all of the characters and allows normal players to revisit the lore of their favorite characters and see how I view all of the characters personally.

The only thing I can really point to as being notable on my page is that I was able to get a drop down menu to replace the previous navigation bar when my page goes from desktop view to phone view. I'm happy with the way it came out.

The only bad thing I can point out currently is that during validation there are a lot of warnings for not having headings, but I personally don't think they're needed where the validator says they should be. It really wants headings to be placed at the start of every section, despite most not needing them and the sections being there for semantic purposes. Other than that I can't think of much wrong with the page off the top of my head. I hope you enjoyed viewing it.
