The Plague Doctor's Starting Skills

  • Noxious Blast:
    Usable in ranks 2-4, targeting targets 1-2.
    Deals 2-3 damage and applies 4 Blight

  • Blinding Gas:
    Usable in ranks 3-4, targeting both targets 3-4.
    Applies Blind and possibly Combo

  • Incision:
    Usable in ranks 1-3, targeting targets 1-2.
    Deals 3-6 damage and applies 3 Bleed

  • Battlefield Medicine:
    Usable in ranks 3-4, targeting a single ally
    Heals a target under 50% HP by 20% and removes Blight, Bleed, and Burn

  • Ounce of Prevention:
    Usable in ranks 3-4, targeting all allies
    Increases resistance to Blight, Bleed, Burn, and Disease by 15%
    Has a cooldown of three turns
