The Plague Doctor

"Indiscriminate science stains the surgeon's hands."

The Plague Doctor, real name Paracelsus, was an academic who had an uncanny focus on the life and death of all things, especially people. Her unconventional theories brought the ire of her classmates and professor, who would mock and jeer at Paracelsus. She would come to debate her professor regarding the blandness and useless lecture, but despite her best efforts she was unable to win over the opinion of her professor or her classmates. One day she finds the professor slumped over the lecture podium and eager to prove her theories and having a fresh cadaver she proceeded to demonstrate her abilities in front of her class. She had successfully taken apart and reanimated the dead professor, but in doing so she terrified her classmates, whose screams still reverberate within her mind.

Flagellant's
Starting
Skills

  • Punish:
    Usable in ranks 2-3, targeting targets 1-2.
    Deals 3-6 damage and applies 3 Blight.
    Self takes 10% HP damage

  • Fester:
    Usable in ranks 1-4, targeting target corpse.
    Clear corpse and give adjacent targets 3 Blight

  • Deathless:
    Usable in ranks 1-4, targeting a single ally.
    Heals a target under 100% HP by 25%.
    Self takes 20% HP damage.
    Has a cooldown of 1 turn

  • Endure:
    Usable in ranks 1-4, targeting a single ally
    Remove 2 Stress from target and add 2 Stress to self

  • Lash's Gift:
    Usable in ranks 2-4, targeting a single ally
    Add 1 Strength, Block, and Stress to target, but heal 25% HP to self if HP is under 50%
    Has a cooldown of 2 turns
