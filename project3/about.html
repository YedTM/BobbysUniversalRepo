'Til The Very Bookend

High Concept: Fight through enemies to reach your favorite book and try to make it out in one piece.

Genre: Adventure, Shooter

Platform: Desktop web

Story: You play as Laslow the Magus, who is a bit obsessed with collecting books and tomes. He hears of a super rare edition of his favorite novel, "Troglodyte's Guide to Book Collecting", and needs to get his hands on it immediately. The only issue, the book is guarded by a lot of sentinels and Laslow will have to fight or avoid them in order to get his desired book.

Aesthetics: The graphic style, for both visuals and sound, will most likely resemble 8-bit games (less detail, but can still tell what things are meant to be).

Gameplay: The gameplay will be moving around the play area space by space using WASD and using the C key to pick up items. WASD and Space will be used during combat to move and attack respectively. The player will be told these controls at the start screen. The first combat will be unavoidable, but will be easier than the others to ease the player in.
